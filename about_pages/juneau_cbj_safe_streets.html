City and Borough of Juneau’s Safe Streets for All Interactive Comment Map

Welcome to the City and Borough of Juneau’s Safe Streets for All (CBJ SS4A) Interactive Comment Map!

The CBJ needs your help identifying transportation safety concerns.
Use this comment map to:
  • Share your experiences using the transportation system and recommend safety upgrades.
  • Learn about and share your level of support for what other community members have experienced or recommend.
    • The comment map is open through January 31,2025. Thank you for contributing your knowledge and experiences to the SS4A Plan! Your responses will help inform future transportation safety actions and infrastructure in the CBJ!

    Map Instructions:

    1. Get Familiar with the Map:
      • Click and drag the map to move around.
      • Use the “+” or “-” buttons in the lower right-hand corner to zoom in or out.
      • Click the view options in the upper right-hand corner to see different basemaps and layers.
    2. Check Out and Respond to Others’ Comments:
      • Make sure the box in the upper right-hand corner has a check next to “Public Comments”.
      • Find a comment you agree with or support? Click the “thumb up”.
    3. Submit a New Comment:
      • Click on the “Add a Comment” button in upper right-hand corner.
      • Follow the instructions to “Click here to enter information OR drag the marker to adjust location.”
      • Write and submit a comment about a safety, connectivity or another issue impacting the transportation network.
