Welcome to the City of Homer Comprehensive Plan Rewrite Comment Map

We need your feedback! We've been listening and we want to get it right as we develop the revised Homer Comprehensive Plan. The revised plan will be a combination of long-term vision, goals, and practical strategies that will guide decisions about land use, public facilities and services, economic development, housing, related infrastructure, programs, and more. It will provide a roadmap for implementation, with clear priorities and actions.

Instructions

Get Familiar with the Map:

1.      Click and drag the map to move around.

2.      Use the "+" or "-" buttons in the lower right-hand corner to zoom in or out.

3.      Click the view options in the upper right corner to see different basemaps and layers.

Check Out and Respond to Others' Comments:

1.      Make sure the box in the upper right corner has a check next to "Public Comments".

2.      Find a comment you agree with/support? Click the "thumb up".

Submit a New Comment:

1.      Click on the "Add a Comment" button in the upper right-hand corner.

2.      Follow the instructions to "Click here to enter information OR drag the marker to adjust in what location you want to add a comment.

3.      Write and submit a comment about something you would like to preserve or change about that location, or other comments/questions.

4.      Please be respectful in your comments. Any inappropriate comments, including use of profanity or references to specific individuals, will be removed from the comment map.
